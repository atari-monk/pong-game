## Pong Ball Draft

### Simplest Pong Ball Behavior

* Spawn in the center.
* Start moving in one direction.
* Move in a straight line.
* Bounce off the top wall.
* Bounce off the bottom wall.
* Bounce off paddles.
* Keep moving after each bounce.
* If it leaves the left side, the right player scores.
* If it leaves the right side, the left player scores.
* Reset to the center after a score.
* Launch again after resetting.
